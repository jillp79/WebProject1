User stories

Potential rabbit adopter:
As a potential rabbit adopter, I want to learn about rabbit care and see rabbits for sale with prices, so that I can make an informed decision on what rabbit I want to purchase.


Rabbit breeder:
As a rabbit breeder, I want to find information on rabbits available with pricing and pedigrees, so that I can review genetics and breeding of the rabbits before I make a selection to purchase.


Rabbit owner:
As a rabbit owner, I want to find useful information about rabbit care, proper housing, and nutrition so that I can take proper care of my rabbit.
